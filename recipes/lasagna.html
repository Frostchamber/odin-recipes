Lasagna Recipe

Making lasagna can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait. You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below, but let's go over the basics:

If you want to stay true to the original recipe, though, these are the ingredients you'll need to add to your grocery list:

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato Products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheese
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricottta mixture
  4. Cover foil and bake
  5. Let lasagna rest before serving
